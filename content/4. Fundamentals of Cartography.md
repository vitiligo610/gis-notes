## Why Make Maps?
Before creating a map, ask yourself:
*   What is the purpose of the map?
*   What information are you trying to communicate?
*   To whom are you communicating?
*   *Custom map info is often purposefully: omitted, simplified, or exaggerated.*

## Essential Map Elements
To build an effective layout, always include:
1.  Border or Neat Line / Frame
2.  North Arrow (or Graticule)
3.  Correct & Appropriate Scale
4.  Legend
5.  Title *(Pro-tip: Do not include the actual word "map" in the title)*
6.  Projection Information (Type used, Central Meridian, Standard Parallels)
7.  Name of the Cartographer (or Agency)
8.  Source & Date of the Data
9.  Date the map was created
10. Always arrange elements in an effective layout.
